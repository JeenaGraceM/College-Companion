Tech Club

A student-run community for learning, building, and sharing technology projects.

Our Mission

The Tech Club empowers students to explore modern technology through hands-on learning, collaborative projects, and peer mentoring. We focus on practical skills — from web & mobile development to AI, robotics, and systems programming — and encourage experimentation, open source contribution, and teamwork.

What we do

  • Weekly meetups: Talks, lightning demos, and mini-hands-on sessions run by members and alumni.
  • Project teams: Build real projects — web apps, games, bots, and embedded systems — with peer support and code reviews.
  • Hackathons & challenges: Organised on campus or online; team up and ship prototypes in 24–48 hours.
  • Career prep: Resume clinics, mock interviews, and tech talks with industry guests.

How to get involved

Everyone is welcome — whether you’re just starting or already building complex systems. Join a project, attend a workshop, or propose an idea for a club event.

Pro tip: Bring a laptop, curiosity, and at least one idea you want to build.
