Our Mission

The Drama Club is a space for students who love acting, storytelling, and stagecraft. We encourage creativity, collaboration, and confidence through stage performances, scriptwriting, and theatrical workshops. Our aim is to provide a platform for every voice to be heard and every talent to shine.

What we do

  • Annual productions: Full-length stage plays directed and performed by members.
  • Workshops: Acting, voice training, body language, and stage presence sessions.
  • Street performances: Socially relevant plays performed on campus and beyond.
  • Scriptwriting & storytelling: Develop original scripts and adapt classics.

How to get involved

All students are welcome — whether you’re a seasoned performer or simply curious about theatre. Audition for plays, help backstage, or contribute to scriptwriting sessions.

Pro tip: Bring your energy, creativity, and the courage to step into the spotlight!
