Debate Club

A platform to sharpen critical thinking, communication, and leadership through debate.

Our Mission

The Debate Club is a forum for students to engage in thoughtful discussions, express diverse perspectives, and refine their skills in public speaking and argumentation. We believe that healthy debate fosters confidence, awareness, and respect for differing viewpoints.

What we do

  • Weekly debates: Structured debates on current affairs, philosophy, and social issues.
  • Workshops: Training in rhetoric, body language, persuasion, and rebuttal techniques.
  • Competitions: Represent the college in intercollegiate debate and Model UN events.
  • Discussion forums: Open sessions for exchanging ideas and building confidence in speaking.

How to get involved

All students are welcome — whether you’re a seasoned speaker or looking to overcome stage fright. Join our weekly meetings, participate in debates, or contribute to workshops and training sessions.

Pro tip: Come with curiosity, an open mind, and a strong voice — every opinion matters here!
